Frequently Asked Questions

Some FAQs to help you

Why should I sign up/log in?

We offer helpful features like favorites, review, add a property, personal recommendations. In order to avail and make full use of these brilliant features, we require that you make an account on our website.

Why do I need to add a description while making an account?

The description provide by you about your preferences while making an account will help us to serve you better by using our dynamic algorithms that recommend houses according to your preferences.

Why can’t I add a property to favorites?

Go to the “log in” button at the top right of the page and make sure that you have logged into your account.

Where can I see the properties that I favorite?

Go to “profile” to open your user profile. Click on the “favorited properties” tab to open the list.

How do I look at the properties?

On the homepage, click of “Property List” or go to property on the top panel of the site.

Why can’t I add review to a property?

Go to the “log in” button at the top right of the page and make sure that you have logged into your account.

How to add a property to sell/rent?

Log in to the website using your credentials by clicking on the “log in” button. Go to “profile” and click “Submit new property” to add a property for sale/rent.

How to compare properties?

Click on “compare” under the “properties” section. Select 2 properties to compare.

Can I remove a property from my favorites?

You can do this by going to “listed properties” and then remove the favorite sign corresponding to the property you want to remove from the favorite list.

Can I remove a property from sale/rent?

Go to “profile” and select “my properties” to view properties listed/added by you. Here, you will see a delete button that will allow you to remove a property from your listings.

Who are these agents? When do I contact them?

You can find our agents under the “about” section. All of our agents all experts in property analysis. These agents are professionally hired by us in order to assist you in buying the best home.
